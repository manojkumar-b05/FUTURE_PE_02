# Target Audience

## Primary Audience

### College Students
Age: 18–24

Needs:
- Affordable earbuds
- Music
- Gaming
- Online classes

Challenges:
- Limited budget
- Need long battery life

---

## Secondary Audience

### Working Professionals
Age: 22–35

Needs:
- Office calls
- Video meetings
- Comfortable earbuds

Challenges:
- Background noise
- Battery backup

---

## Third Audience

### Fitness Enthusiasts
Age: 18–35

Needs:
- Workout music
- Sweat resistance
- Secure fit

Challenges:
- Earbuds falling out
- Low durability
